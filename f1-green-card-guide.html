1
Initial Assessment
Immediately upon notification
Confirm the pending I-485 application via I-797C receipt notice. Document the student's written intent not to enroll and to pursue Adjustment of Status instead.
⚠️ Advise student: No work authorization, international travel, or F-1 benefits while AOS is pending and SEVIS is active.

Monitor Enrollment Deadline
Ongoing until session start
Do not manually terminate the SEVIS record. Allow the system to auto-terminate for "Failure to Enroll" approximately 90 days after the next session start date if the student does not register.

3
Pre-Termination Actions
Before auto-termination occurs
Issue a school letter to the student covering all relevant status points.
  • Confirms F-1 status maintained to date of non-enrollment
  • Documents student's voluntary decision not to enroll
  • Notes pending AOS (I-485) filed with USCIS
  • States no further F-1 obligations exist post-termination
✓ Note: The student remains in an authorized period of stay while the AOS petition is pending — regardless of SEVIS status.

Post Auto-Termination
Day of termination + 15-day grace period
SEVIS ends the student's F-1 status upon auto-termination. A 15-day grace period follows, during which the student must either depart the U.S. or maintain AOS coverage.

Risks & Ongoing Guidance
